Ban Policy / Limitation Note
Date: 2026-03-11

Current status:
- Kick is implemented.
- Persistent ban is not implemented in this phase.

Rationale:
A correct ban mechanism requires a stable notion of participant identity across reconnects.
The current implementation supports reconnect as a fresh new client connection with a new client id.
Because identity-preserving reconnect is not implemented, a robust ban policy would be underspecified.

What is implemented instead:
- The current controller may remove another currently connected client using the kick command.
- Observer clients are not allowed to use kick.
- After kick, the removed client may reconnect later as a fresh new observer connection.

Conclusion:
For this phase, kick is implemented as the practical administrative control action.
Persistent ban is left as future work rather than implemented incompletely.
